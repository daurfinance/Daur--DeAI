- Series B investors receive $75M before common shareholders

**Anti-dilution Protection:**

**Voting Rights:**
- 1 share = 1 vote
- Protective provisions for major decisions

**Information Rights:**
- Quarterly financial statements

- Monthly KPI dashboard

**Board Representation:**
- Seed lead: 1 board seat
- Series A lead: 1 board observer seat
- Series B lead: 1 board seat

**Drag-Along Rights:**
- Majority shareholders can force sale

**Tag-Along Rights:**

- Same terms as majority

- Right to participate in future rounds
- Pro-rata basis
